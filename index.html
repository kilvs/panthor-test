Explore Panthor Configurations

Panthor: the sophisticated, uncompromising strength system engineered for athletes who demand flexibility, style, and unrivalled ergonomics in their pursuit of mastery and victory.

Bolt Down

Our most compact setup, measuring just 1.8m x 0.3m with only 1m of operational space required at the front. Ideal for maximum floor space efficiency.

Freestanding

A retrofittable platform measuring 1.8m x 1m, designed with tapered edges for accessibility and a sleek aesthetic. Compatible with the Panthor bespoke bench, but also fully functional as a standalone unit for use with your own equipment, or none at all.

Bench

Custom-built to pair with the Panthor system. Removable and stowable, with three adjustable pads for versatile support across a wide range of exercises: seated leg curls, 45° leg press, preacher curls, lying hamstring curl, supported kickbacks, back extensions, incline/decline bench press, sit-ups, and more.
